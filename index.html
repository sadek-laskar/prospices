Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Perfected by Passion

Founded in 2026 in the heart of Bangalore, ProSpices began as a small family venture with one simple belief: that pure spices should never be a luxury. Today, we source directly from over 200 partner farms across Bangalore, Gujarat, Kerala, and Andhra Pradesh.

Every batch travels through our FSSAI-certified processing unit where it is cleaned, graded, slow-ground in controlled temperatures, and triple-tested before it reaches your kitchen. No fillers, no artificial colours, no compromises — just the real thing.

From households in Jaipur to five-star hotel chains in Mumbai, our spices flavour over 5,000 kitchens every day. Our modern vacuum-sealed packaging preserves freshness for up to 24 months without any preservatives.

Contact Our Team
Are ProSpices products 100% pure with no additives?+
Yes, absolutely. All ProSpices products are 100% pure with zero artificial colours, flavour enhancers, anti-caking agents, or preservatives. We are FSSAI-certified and our facility undergoes regular third-party audits to ensure compliance with the highest food safety standards.
What is the shelf life of ProSpices products?+
Our vacuum-sealed packs maintain freshness and potency for up to 24 months from the date of manufacture when stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, we recommend using within 3–4 months and storing in an airtight container.
Do you supply in bulk for restaurants and hotels?+
Yes! We have dedicated B2B pricing for restaurants, hotels, cloud kitchens, caterers, and food businesses. Minimum order quantities start at 5 kg per SKU. Contact our sales team for a customised quote, white-labelling options, and delivery schedules.
How are the spices processed to retain their natural flavour?+
We use controlled low-temperature stone-grinding rather than high-speed industrial milling. This preserves the essential oils and volatile aromatic compounds that give spices their flavour and colour. The temperature during grinding never exceeds 45°C to avoid degradation of active compounds.
Do you offer private-label or white-label packaging?+
Yes, we offer comprehensive white-label solutions for businesses looking to sell spices under their own brand. We handle everything from formulation to custom packaging design, label printing, and quality certification. Minimum order applies — contact us for details.
Which cities do you deliver to?+
We currently deliver across 20+ cities including Jaipur, Delhi, Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, Kolkata, Surat, Lucknow, and more. Pan-India shipping is available for online orders. Contact us to check availability in your area.
Are ProSpices products certified and tested?+
All ProSpices products carry FSSAI certification. Our processing facility is ISO 22000:2018 compliant. Every batch is tested for pesticide residue, heavy metals, microbial contamination, and moisture content by accredited third-party laboratories before release.
